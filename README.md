### Cookie Valley is a fun career simulator made in Roblox. Pick between 4 jobs - farming, warehouse, delivery, and baking - and earn cash for playing as each job. Grow crops, sort ingredients, deliver goods, and bake cookies while learning about jobs on the way.

- **Farmer**: Grow crops, gather eggs & milk, and deliver harvests.
- **Warehouse Worker**: Sort items on a conveyor into correct boxes and deliver them for rewards.
- **Delivery Driver**: Follow a delivery route as fast as possible and unload the truck.
- **Baker**: Take orders, gather ingredients, bake cookies, and serve customers efficiently.

## Purpose

Cookie Valley allows players to explore careers and gain real world skills. Learn how different jobs work, develop task management skills, and enjoy interactive gameplay without real stress!
